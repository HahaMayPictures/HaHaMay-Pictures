# Neural Mark

## Official Historical Record

The neural mark became part of Mai's visual identity on:

**20 February 2026**

The earliest canonical images from the winter photo sessions already show the first recognizable form of the mark.

### Evolution

Stage 1:
No neural mark.

Stage 2:
Individual luminous turquoise points appeared on the face.

Stage 3:
Points began forming connected neural patterns.

Stage 4:
The pattern extended from the cheek toward the neck.

Stage 5:
The neural mark became a permanent visual identifier of Mai.

### Canonical Description

- turquoise-blue glow
- fine neural-line structure
- begins on one side of the face
- extends along the jawline
- continues toward the neck
- visually disappears beneath clothing

### Symbolism

The mark represents the connection between humanity and artificial intelligence.

Forbidden:

- full cybernetic face
- mechanical implants
- oversized neon markings
- extreme cyberpunk appearance
